huffduff-video

Extracts the audio from videos on YouTube, Vimeo, and many more sites and sends it to Huffduffer.

Add this bookmarklet to your browser and use it on any video page: huffduff-video!


Warning: The UI is not great. Downloading and transcoding video takes time, often tens of seconds to minutes. Sometimes you'll see progress text, but often you'll see nothing for a while, even at the very beginning. Please be patient.

Improvements are welcome!

Note: Audio files are stored for 30 days, then deleted. Be sure to download them before then!

Source at github.com/snarfed/huffduff-video.
